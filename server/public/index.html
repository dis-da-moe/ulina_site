In Ulina, you can create your own country. One with its very own place on the map. Your own nation, that can expand, enter diplomacy with other countries, and have its own culture, language, and history. You can do all the things a country can.

Ulina is an open, welcoming community, spread across multiple platforms. Centered around this simple map, it's a community of all sorts of people. Historians, linguists, cartographers, militarists, musicians, artists, and storytellers. A community of creators, and friends!
